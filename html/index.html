Stories From México

A Mexican Cinema & Culture Week in Ireland

September 7th - 11th, 2026 | Light House Cinema, Dublin

Explore Program
About Us

Who We Are

The Stories from Mexico festival is proposed as the bridge connecting both countries, facilitating and generating greater global exposure for Mexican filmmakers.

Our goal is to support the local economy and the Mexican community living in Ireland in a simple and direct way, while also offering Irish artists a platform to exhibit their work in Mexico.

Our Objective

To promote the cultural richness and diversity of Mexico in Ireland through cinema and other artistic expressions, strengthening the cultural ties between both countries during the Mexican Independence celebrations.

5 Days

100% In-Person Event

1 Feature & 1 Short Film Daily

Festival Format

Daily Screenings

Enjoy 1 Mexican feature film and 1 short film per day, featuring brief introductions by cast and crew member.

Cultural Receptions

Opening and closing ceremonies featuring traditional Mexican food, drinks, music, and dance performances like Calpulli Mictlán.

Arts & Exhibitions

Daily exhibitions showcasing photography and paintings created by talented Mexican artists.

FAQs

It is a cultural week in Dublin dedicated to showcasing the richness of cinema and arts. Our festival serves as a bridge between Mexico and Ireland, featuring film screenings, art exhibitions, and gastronomic experiences.
The 2026 edition will be held from September 7th to September 11th. The main screenings will take place at the Light House Cinema, Smithfield, Dublin.
