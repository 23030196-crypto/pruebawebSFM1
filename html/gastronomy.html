Mexican Gastronomy

The opening and closing nights feature traditional Mexican food and drink receptions — a full sensory celebration of Mexican culture.

🌮

Opening Night Reception

September 7 — A traditional Mexican food and drinks welcome at the Light House Cinema foyer. Free for all ticket holders.

🍹

Networking Night

September 9 — Food, music, and drinks during the México & Ireland networking evening for artists and filmmakers.

🎉

Closing Ceremony Dinner

September 11 — Grand closing night with traditional Mexican cuisine, mezcal, music, and the raffle draw.

Mexican Restaurants in Dublin

We partner with Mexican-owned and Mexican-inspired restaurants in Dublin to extend the festival experience beyond the cinema. Look for our SFM partner badge at participating venues during festival week.
