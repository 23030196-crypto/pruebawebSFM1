Community Context

Dublin's Latin American & Spanish-speaking community surpassed 55,000 residents in 2025.

Approximately 7% are Mexican — a community showing consistent growth in recent years.

Despite growing demand for Mexican content, distribution challenges have kept Mexican productions largely absent from Irish screens — until now.

Festival Benefits

Cultural Representation

Greater visibility for Mexican culture and arts in the heart of Europe, aligned with Independence Day celebrations.

Local Community

Actively involves Mexican groups already established in Ireland — gastronomy, cinema, dance, photography, painting and music.

Global Filmmakers

Gives Mexican filmmakers living in Mexico and around the world expanded exposure to international audiences.

Irish Artists

Opens a gateway for Irish artists to exhibit their work in Mexico — a bridge to the Latin American community.

Institutional Event

A ceremonial and cultural event aligned with Mexican national celebrations, with Embassy institutional endorsement.

Artistic Integration

Integrates traditional Mexican artistic and gastronomic expressions, creating a fully immersive cultural experience.
