Open Call 2026

Submit Your Work

We welcome Mexican and Mexico-connected filmmakers, photographers, and visual artists to submit their work for the 2026 edition. Submissions are managed via FilmFreeway.

Feature Films

Mexican feature films over 60 min. Drama, comedy, documentary — all genres welcome.

Short Films

Mexican short films under 40 min. Fiction, documentary, animation, and experimental formats.

Photography

Photo series or individual works by Mexican photographers. Exhibited daily during the festival.

Visual Arts

Paintings, illustrations, and mixed media by Mexican visual artists for the daily exhibition space.

Submission Guidelines

  • ✓Film must be directed or co-directed by a Mexican national or a director with strong Mexican ties.
  • ✓Films may be in Spanish or any Mexican indigenous language. English subtitles required.
  • ✓Co-productions with Ireland or other countries are especially welcome for Days 2 & 3.
  • ✓Film must have been completed after January 2023.
  • ✓Visual art submissions must include high-resolution images and artist statement.
